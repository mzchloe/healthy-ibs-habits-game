Healthy Habits for Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS)

Have you met Bella? She has been suffering from major constipation and diarrhea and feels like she's 9-months pregnant almost all the time. But it does not have to be that way, Bella can live a symptom-free lifestyle as long as she can establish some healthy habits.


She loves food with onions, drinking and takes a lot of smoking breaks due to her stress, this creates so much pain for her. But with just a few simple habit changes, like water, sleep, eating healthy fruits like kiwi and doing regular exercises can help her improve her health again.
